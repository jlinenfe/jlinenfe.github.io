Hi, I'm Josh

Career Overview

I’m an environmental scientist with a growing passion for data science and a strong drive to solve real-world problems through data driven insights. With a background in Environmental Science, I bring a combination of analytical expertise and technical skills that translate complex data into actionable insights.

I manage and analyze large-scale ecological datasets to support data-driven decision-making for environmental policy and restoration efforts at the state level. My work includes cleaning and organizing complex data, building reproducible workflows, and developing custom graphics and dashboards that communicate trends clearly to scientists, stakeholders, and decision makers. Using tools like R, Excel, and ArcPro, I help translate raw environmental data into actionable insights that guide planning and conservation across South Florida.

I’m committed to continuous learning and thrive on projects that drive clarity and efficiency. My goal is to help businesses organize, clean, and streamline their data to uncover insights that lead to informed decision making and better performance. Whether it's optimizing workflows, automating reporting, or analyzing key trends, I aim to deliver value through thoughtful problem-solving, collaboration, and a strong analytical foundation.

Hobbies
